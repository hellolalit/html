Ch4 - Paragraph in Html5

Paragraph Tag -

Paragraph in HTML5 are used a description to descript about something. It is made up of content — text, images, or other content like form fields — that appears between an opening tag and closing tag. Paragraphs belong to the class of elements called block-level elements.
